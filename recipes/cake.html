Cake recipe

This decadent chocolate drip cake combines soft layers of moist chocolate sponge with creamy cookies-and-cream frosting, topped with a glossy dark chocolate ganache

Decorated with Oreos, KitKat bars, and malted chocolate balls, it’s a show-stopping dessert that balances rich flavors and textures perfectly.

Ingredients

cake

  • 2 cups of flour
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 1½ tsp baking powder
  • 1½ tsp baking soda
  • 1¾ cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup oil
  • 1 cup hot water
  • 1 tsp vanilla extra
  • pinch of salt(lol)

Frosting

  • 1½ cups butter
  • 3 cups powdered sugar
  • ½ cup crushed Oreos
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Ganache

  • 1 cup dark chocolate chips
  • ½ cup heavy cream

Decoration

  • Oreos
  • KitKat bars
  • malted chocolate balls
  • crushed cookies,
  • sprinkles

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F). Mix dry cake ingredients; add eggs, milk, oil, vanilla, then hot water. Bake in two 8-inch pans for 30–35 min; cool completely
  2. Beat butter for frosting; add sugar gradually, then milk, vanilla, and crushed Oreos. Frost the cake evenly
  3. For ganache, heat cream until simmering; pour over chocolate chips, stir until smooth, let cool slightly, then drip over the cake edges.
  4. Top with Oreos, KitKats, malt balls, and cookie crumbs. Chill for 30 min before serving.
